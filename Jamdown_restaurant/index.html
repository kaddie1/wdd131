Jamdown Restaurant

Jamaica is an island in the Caribbean that is surrounded by the Caribbean Sea. It is also referred to as the “Land of Wood and Water”. It gained its independence on August 6, 1962. Because of its history, they have a rich and diverse culture. Its motto is “Out of Many One People.” The population as of 2022 is 2.83 million. The Jamaican flag is black, green, and gold.

The purpose of Jamdown Restaurant is to provide scrumptious Jamaican food such as ackee and saltfish and rice and peas. This website is for anyone who wants to delve into Jamaican food and for those who dreams of visiting Jamaica but don’t have the resources to go there.We hope we will be able to accomplish our purpose of you experiencing a little of Jamaica. We also offer a variety of dishes.
